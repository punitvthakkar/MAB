Welcome! Test your decision-making by choosing levers to maximize your score.

  • You'll face Red, Blue, and Green levers.
  • Each lever has a fixed but hidden reward probability for this game session.
  • These probabilities are determined by the difficulty you select.
  • Your goal: Discover the most rewarding lever through exploration and exploitation.
  • You have 25 pulls to score as many +1 points as possible.
  • The game takes about 2 minutes.

This game helps understand how you approach uncertain choices.
